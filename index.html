About YNWA

Connecting Global Talent with Opportunity

Established in 2011 and headquartered in Kuwait, YNWA has grown into a trusted international recruitment agency with branch offices in Qatar, Nepal, India, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, and Kenya—alongside partner networks across Africa, Eastern Europe, and Southeast Asia.

We’ve earned a reputation for excellence by consistently delivering top-tier talent to employers across the Middle East, Malaysia, and Europe. Our clients value our global reach, streamlined sourcing process, and personalized approach to recruitment.

With deep insight into multiple industries and workforce demands, we provide end-to-end solutions—from sourcing to deployment—ensuring a smooth, hassle-free experience for both employers and candidates. Whether you're looking to build your career or grow your organization, YNWA is here to guide you every step of the way.

Recruitment Process and Deployment Timeline

Steps we follow to recruit manpower from overseas

  • Approach the employer company and offer the business proposal
  • Formulate all the terms of the deal and finalize them
  • Confirm the business deal with the employer’s company
  • Sign the business contract

  • Coordinate job order details with the employer
  • Prepare and verify job order for visa approval
  • Get attestation from relevant authorities
  • Send job order to branch or associate offices abroad

  • Define selection criteria and process
  • Source CVs and conduct preliminary interviews
  • Arrange interviews via in-person, video, or phone
  • Prepare final selection list and hand over to employer
  • Coordinate job offers and reference checks
  • Collect signed offer letters, medicals, and visa docs
  • Continue coordination until joining

  • Collect visa from employer company
  • Confirm flight schedule
  • Deploy the selected candidate to the destination country
Recruitment

Find the Right Talent Through Our Streamlined Hiring Process

What does recruitment involve?

The hiring process ensures that the right candidates are selected efficiently and professionally. It typically includes the following phases, all aimed at identifying and attracting the best talent:

  • Workforce Planning & Identifying Needs
  • Job Description Development
  • Strategic Job Posting & Promotion
  • Application Review & Shortlisting
  • Candidate Communication & Coordination
Learn More
How do we assess and select the right candidates?

Interviews are a crucial part of evaluating whether a candidate is the right fit for both the role and the organization’s culture. Our procedure includes:

  • Initial Screening (Phone/Video Call)
  • Technical or Role-Specific Interview
  • Behavioral & Situational Questions
  • Panel or Peer Interviews (if required)
  • Final Review & Feedback Loop
Learn More
How do we match candidates to the right opportunities?

Requirement matching ensures candidates meet both the job criteria and company expectations. Our strategy focuses on:

  • Analyzing Job Requirements & Key Competencies
  • Matching Technical Skills & Qualifications
  • Assessing Cultural Fit & Career Goals
  • Verifying Certifications & Work Experience
  • Aligning Expectations on Role, Pay, and Growth
